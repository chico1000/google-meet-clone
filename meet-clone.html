Can external users participate in a video call?

Yes. With the free version of Google Meet, all participants must sign in with a Google account or request to join the meeting from the person who organized the video conference. Google Workspace customers can also invite participants without a Google account to video meetings. Simply share the link or meeting ID with everyone invited.

How much does Google Meet cost?

Anyone with a Google account can create a video conference with up to 100 participants for free. The maximum duration per conference is 60 minutes. There is no time limit for mobile conferences and 1:1 calls. If you want to hold longer meetings with a larger number of participants and use additional features such as international dial-in numbers, video conference recording, live streaming and management functions, you can find more information on theSubscriptions and prices for organizationsas well as underGoogle Workspace Individual.
